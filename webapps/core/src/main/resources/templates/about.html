About BioSamples

The team

The BioSamples team is part of the Tony Burdett's team at EMBL-EBI. The team consists of bioinformaticians, ontologists, software engineers, and web developers.

Contact us

If you want to contact us, please email biosamples@ebi.ac.uk. We welcome enquiries on data submission and access, as well as technical issues like integration with other databases and linked open data. We aim to respond within 1 working day.

Announcements

If you use the BioSamples Database regularly, or maintain software that interacts with it, you may want to stay updated by subscribing to our announcement mailing list.

Publications / How to Cite

  • Citing BioSamples database: Please use the following publication: Courtot M, Gupta D, Liyanage I, Xu F, Burdett T. BioSamples database: FAIRer samples metadata to accelerate research data management. Nucleic Acids Research. 2021 Nov 8. https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkab1046
  • Citing BioSamples records in your manuscript: Please include your sample and group accession numbers and the URL to the BioSamples homepage (e.g., "Sample metadata are available in the BioSamples database (http://www.ebi.ac.uk/biosamples) under accession numbers SAMxxxxxx, SAMxxxxxx, SAMxxxxxx, and SAMxxxxxx.").
  • Previous publications: Melanie Courtot et al. BioSamples database: an updated sample metadata hub, Nucleic Acids Research, 2019, https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gky1061.
