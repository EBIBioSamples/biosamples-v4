About BioSamples

The team

The BioSamples team is part of the Helen Parkinson's team at EMBL-EBI. The team consists of bioinformaticians, ontologists, software engineers and web developers.

Contact us

If you want to contact us, please email biosamples@ebi.ac.uk. We welcome enquiries on data submission and data access, as well as more technical issues, e.g. integration with other data bases, linked open data, etc. We aim to respond to all emails within 1 working day.

Announcements

If you use the Biosamples Database regularly, or maintain software that interacts with it, you may want to keep up to date with any changes and improvements by subscribing to the announcement mailing list.

Publications / how to cite

  • Citing BioSamples database: please use the following publication: Faulconbridge A., et al., 2014. Updates to BioSamples database at European Bioinformatics Institute. Nucleic Acids Res, 10.1093/nar/gkt1081. Pubmed ID 24265224.
  • Citing BioSamples records in your manuscript: please include your sample and group accession numbers and the URL to BioSamples home page e.g. "Sample meta-data are available in the BioSamples database (http://www.ebi.ac.uk/biosamples) under accession numbers SAMxxxxxx, SAMxxxxxx, SAMxxxxxx, and SAMxxxxxx."
